WELCOME TO WESTERN HACKNIGHTS


Come hang out with us! We host workshops, lend out hardware, and spend time helping each other out on tech-based projects.

Whether you're a total newbie or an advanced tinkerer, whether you're interested in web, mobile, or hardware, we'll have something for you.

Location and Time


We meet on Thursdays at 6:30PM in Middlesex College Room 325.

Join our Facebook group to stay updated on events: Western Hack Nights

Hardware Lab


Check out all our cool hardware! Hardware is available for lending every Thursday.

If you would like to take the hardware home for use in a project, please email Anoop (atoor25@uwo.ca) and Ruth (rwong233@uwo.ca)

and your request will be approved on a case by case basis.
